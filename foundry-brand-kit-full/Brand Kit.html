02 · Clear space

Give the mark room

Maintain at least ⅓ of the icon's width of clear space on every side. Don't crowd it — the corners do work.

Minimum sizing

The mark holds up well at small sizes thanks to the bold counters in the F and P, but enforce a hard floor:

  • Digital: never below 24×24 px (use the simplified favicon below 32 px).
  • Print: never below 12 mm wide for the icon, 30 mm for the lockup.
  • Wordmark: 80 px or 20 mm minimum to keep the period readable.
  • Backgrounds: always pair with #0B0C0F or a near-black solid; never on busy photos without a scrim.
03 · Color

A single hot accent

Amber 500 is the hero. Use it sparingly — links, primary actions, the period in the wordmark. Click any swatch to copy its hex.

07 · Usage

        
Do & don't

      

      
The mark is geometric and intentional. Don't soften the corners, recolor it, or stretch it to fit. When in doubt, use the SVG at native ratio.

    


    

      

        

          Do
          
        

        
Native ratio on dark surface. The mark was designed for #0B0C0F.

      

      

        

          Do
          
        

        
Mono on light. Use the 1-color variant when amber is unavailable.

      

      

        

          Do
          
        

        
Respect clear space. ⅓ of the icon's width on all sides.

      


      

        

          Don't
          
        

        
No busy backgrounds. The mark needs a near-black or solid neutral.

      

      

        

          Don't
          
        

        
Never stretch. Maintain the 1:1 aspect ratio at all sizes.

      

      

        

          Don't
          
        

        
Don't recolor. The amber gradient is the brand. Use mono if you need a different hue.
